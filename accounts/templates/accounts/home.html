The Primal Prowess Philosophy

I consider myself to be an "outside of the box" practitioner. I'm a divergent thinker and believe that one cannot design the superior athlete without being unconventional, or otherwise unorthodox, in the training methods implemented. I'm classically educated like most of society. Although I see the relevance in this, I also strongly feel that self-education through experience is just as necessary; this is what truly separates me from others. I believe that these unique, personal experiences have allowed me to extrapolate my own epistemological protocols. One tends to be regarded as "taboo" or “going against the grain” when implementing avant garde methods of performance conditoning. However, xenogamy of principles is pertinent to diversity and innovation within training methodology.

If one desires to achieve a level of athleticism unlike anyone has ever seen, then one must also strive to train like no one else. What will put you at an advantage over the hungry, vicious competition? Your arsenal of athletic abilities must be developmentally sound and devastating to the opposition. Upon subscribing to this site, you will have the resources required to pull back on the throttle of your primal, infinite potential.

Athlematics strives to provide the athlete and coach with viable, relevant knowledge regarding athletic training methodologies via supporting sub-practices such as: kinesiology, psychology, physics, cybernetics, and physiology.
