The Primal Prowess Philosophy

I believe that one cannot design an innovative athlete without being unconventional, or otherwise unorthodox, in the training methods implemented. Coaches tend to be regarded as "taboo" or "going against the grain" by implementing new methods of training. However, this is the path to innovation. One should always keep in mind the previous knowledge they have attained regarding training methodology, but it’s the analytical process of past knowledge that leads to the revolutionary training protocols of today.

If one desires to achieve a level of athleticism unlike anyone has ever seen, then one must also strive to train like no one else. What will put you at an advantage over the hungry, vicious competition? Your arsenal of athletic abilities must be innovative, developmentally sound, and devastating to the opposition. Upon subscribing to this site, you will have the resources required to formulate dynamic, innovative, and relevant athletic training methods; enabling you to pull back on the throttle of your primal, infinite potential.

Athlematics strives to provide the athlete and coach with viable, relevant knowledge regarding athletic training methodologies via supporting sub-practices such as: kinesiology, psychology, physics, cybernetics, and physiology.
