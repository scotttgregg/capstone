The Primal Prowess Philosophy

I consider myself to be an "outside of the box" practitioner. I'm a divergent thinker and believe that one cannot design the superior athlete without being unconventional, or otherwise unorthodox, in the training methods implemented. I'm classically educated like most of society. Although I see the relevance in this, I also strongly feel that self-education through experience is just as necessary; this is what truly separates me from others.

Athlematics strives to provide the athlete and coach with viable, relevant knowledge regarding athletic training methodologies via supporting sub-practices such as: kinesiology, psychology, physics, cybernetics, and physiology.
